Delete Your Account

You can delete your ZoraSync account at any time.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the ZoraSync app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Select "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your request

Alternative Option

You can also request account deletion by email:

privacy@zorasync.com

What Happens Next

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted. This action cannot be undone.
